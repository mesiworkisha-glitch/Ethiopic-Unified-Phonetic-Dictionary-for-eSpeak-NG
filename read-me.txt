Ethiopic Unified Phonetic Dictionary (EUPD)
Author: Meseret Worku Alemu
Version: 1.0 (The 60 KB Gold Master)
Overview
This project represents a massive expansion of the eSpeak-NG Amharic capabilities, moving beyond basic support to a comprehensive, multi-language phonetic engine. It is designed to handle the complex nuances of the Ge'ez script across various Ethiopian and Eritrean languages.
Advanced Features
• 
Homophone Differentiation: Expertly maps traditionally distinct characters (like ሀ, ሐ, ኀ or ሠ, ሰ) to ensure proper phonetic context despite modern pronunciation overlaps.
• 
Traditional Punctuation: Full support for the complete Ge'ez punctuation set, including the word separator (፡), sentence ender (።), and complex marks like the preface (፠) and paragraph separator (፼).
• 
Yaredic (Zema) Signs: Integration of the musical and liturgical notation used in the Ethiopian Orthodox Tewahedo Church, ensuring these signs are recognized during text processing.
• 
Regional Exclusives:
• 
Bilen: Labialized velar nasal series (ⶓ).
• 
Sebat Bet Gurage: Palatalized series (ⶨ, ⶰ) and the heavy alveolar stop (ዸ).
• 
Tigrigna: Support for the ቐ and ጘ families.
• 
Numeral System: Full conversion of Ge'ez numerals from ፩ through ፼.
Technical Specifications
• 
Total Dictionary Entries: 1,446 entries in am_list.
• 
Rule Density: 11 synchronized groups in am_rules.
• 
Binary Size: Optimized to 60 KB for high-performance text-to-speech.

Linguistic Implementation Details
1. Homophone Differentiation & Heritage Spelling
While modern Amharic often pronounces different "S" and "H" characters identically, this Gold Master maintains their traditional phonetic identity for historical and liturgical accuracy:
• 
The "H" Series: Differentiates between ሀ (U+1200), ሐ (U+1228), and ኀ (U+1280).
• 
The "S" Series: Properly maps ሠ (U+1220) and ሰ (U+1238) to ensure scripts that rely on these distinctions are rendered correctly by the speech engine.
• 
The "T's" Series: Distinguishes between ጸ (U+1348) and ፀ (U+1350).
2. Traditional Ge'ez Punctuation
The am_rules file has been optimized to handle the rhythmic pauses required by traditional Ethiopic text:
• 
፡ (Semicolon/Word Space): Handled as a brief breath or separator.
• 
። (Full Stop): Implements a complete falling intonation for sentence termination.
• 
፣ (Comma) and ፤ (Semicolon): Tuned for natural phrasing in long-form Ge'ez documents.
3. Yaredic (Zema) & Liturgical Signs
Unique to this implementation is the support for Yaredic signs, which are essential for ecclesiastical Ethiopian texts:
• 
Musical Notation: Allows the engine to recognize and process the symbols used in the Zema (chant) tradition of the Ethiopian Orthodox Tewahedo Church.
• 
Scriptural Accuracy: Ensures that  religious or historical documents are processed without phonetic errors.

Compilation
Ensure your ph_am, am_list, and am_rules files are in the same directory, then run:
espeak-ng --compile=am
